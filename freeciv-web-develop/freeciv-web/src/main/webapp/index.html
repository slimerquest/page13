Freeciv is a Free and Open Source empire-building strategy game inspired by the history of human civilization. The game commences in prehistory and your mission is to lead your tribe from the Stone Age to the Space Age. Freeciv can be played online in your web-browser for free.

    Free strategy game with these features

    • Playable for free in your HTML5 capable browser, mobile or tablet
    • 3D WebGL or 2D Isometric graphics
    • Artificial Intelligence (AI) computer-controlled players.
    • History lessons from Wikipedia of all technologies, units and buildings.
    • More than 50 playable units and 541 nations.
    • Full Fog of War
    • Freeciv is open source software released under the GNU General Public License
